About YVTU

YVTU (Yutu) is inspired by the Jade Rabbit, a beloved figure in Chinese mythology and modern space exploration.

In ancient Chinese legends, Yutu (pronounced “yù tù”), or the Jade Rabbit, lives on the Moon alongside the Moon goddess Chang’e. This mystical white rabbit is said to be pounding herbs with a mortar and pestle, creating the elixir of life. Over centuries, Yutu has become a symbol of purity, loyalty, and eternal spirit, especially celebrated during the Mid-Autumn Festival.

In modern times, Yutu is also the name of China’s lunar rovers. Yutu-1 (2013) was the first Chinese rover to land on the Moon, and Yutu-2 (2019) became the first to explore the Moon’s far side. These achievements reflect not only technological progress but also the adventurous and persistent spirit that Yutu represents.

Our domain name, YVTU.cn, is a stylized variation of the word Yutu. While the correct pinyin is “yutu,” the spelling “yvtu” gives a modern, unique touch to our brand — and keeps the spirit of the Jade Rabbit alive in a creative way.

We hope YVTU becomes a space where mythology meets modern imagination — where tradition and innovation hop forward, side by side.

YVTU（源自“玉兔”），灵感来自中国古老神话中的吉祥象征——玉兔，也是现代航天探索的代表之一。

在中国传统传说中，玉兔（拼音为 yù tù）与嫦娥一同居住在月宫。传说中，玉兔洁白温顺，在月亮上捣药，炼制长生不老的仙药，象征着纯洁、忠诚与永恒的精神。每逢中秋佳节，人们仰望明月，便会想起这只神秘的月中玉兔。

进入现代，“玉兔”也成为中国探月工程中月球车的名字。2013 年，“玉兔一号”成功登月，开启了中国月球探测的新纪元；2019 年，“玉兔二号”更是成为人类历史上首个登陆月球背面的月球车，象征着探索精神与科技进步。

我们的域名 YVTU.cn 是对“玉兔”拼音的创新表达。虽然标准拼音是“yutu”，但“YVTU”作为域名更加独特，具有辨识度，也寓意着我们在保留传统文化精髓的同时，勇于创新与探索。

我们希望 YVTU 成为一个承载想象力的空间，在神话与科技、传统与未来之间，跳跃前行。
